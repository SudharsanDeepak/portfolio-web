During this break, a remastered and condensed version of the Fishman Island arc, titled "One Piece Log: Fishman Island Saga," is airing in its place, highlighting key moments from the Straw Hat Pirates' post-time-skip journey. Upon its return, the anime will continue the Egghead arc, which has already captivated fans since its debut in January 2024. This arc focuses on the Straw Hats' arrival on Egghead Island, the base of Dr. Vegapunk, revealing critical details about the Void Century, advanced technology, and Devil Fruits while showcasing intense confrontations with characters like Kizaru, CP0, and the powerful Seraphim.
